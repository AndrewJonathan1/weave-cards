- **Event Organization**
  - **Life Events**
    - 📍 **Weddings** — High stakes ($10K-$200K budgets), weak tech competition, 12 repeatable pieces, built-in community. 2M weddings/year US. *"Send us your stationery, we'll make your website match"* is the killer entry. Taste extraction is the hard unsolved problem.
    - 📍 **Funerals / Memorials** — Extreme urgency (same-day). Deeply meaningful. ~3M deaths/year US. No one does this well. Funeral homes as distribution channel.
    - Baby showers, Coming of Age (Bar Mitzvahs, Quinceañeras)
  - **Conferences & Events** — Time-sensitive, predictable structure, mostly terrible existing sites. Repeatable per organizer.
  - **Event Venues** — Wedding venues, banquet halls, conference centers. Bookings are $5K-$50K each. Channel play: venue refers couples to your wedding service.
  - Distribution: D2C, Service People (planners, coordinators)
- 📍 **Real Estate** — $2B+ annual web spend
  - **Agents** — 1.5M in US. Personal brand sites. High churn rate (switching brokerages, rebranding). $1.5K-$5K each.
  - **Property Listings** — Speed is critical. A stunning property site same-day as photos? Game-changer. Luxury: $5K-$15K.
  - **New Development** — $5K-$25K per site. Tied to sales launch timelines.
- 📍 **Local Businesses**
  - **Therapists** — The website IS the first therapeutic interaction. Warm, personalized site vs Psychology Today profile. AI: "explore whether this therapist fits your needs." Reduces no-shows.
  - **Medical / Dental** — Credibility sites. Their hourly rate is $300-$1K, so a $3K site is a few hours of billing.
  - **Lawyers** — 450K firms in US. High price tolerance ($3K-$10K).
  - **Restaurants** — Predictable structure, everyone needs one. 60K+ new per year.
- 📍 **Remote Contractors**
  - Coaches, Consultants, Agencies, Marketing Teams
  - *Challenge: coaches often don't have money. Established ones with revenue do. A coach billing $200/hr who gets one new client from the site has paid for it in a week.*
  - **Deliverables:** Pitch Pages, Delivery Portals, Client Deliverables, Microsites
- **Other:** Small businesses, Education, Thought Leaders, Airbnb hosts (direct booking sites), Tour operators
