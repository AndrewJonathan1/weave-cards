### How to evaluate a niche — the 10 properties of a "wedding-shaped" opportunity

1. **Bounded, repeatable deliverable** — Same shape each time. Learnable. Improvable with each rep.
2. **Simple tech ceiling** — AI can produce it reliably. No ongoing engineering complexity.
3. **High emotional + financial stakes** — People care deeply AND have money already allocated.
4. **Weak tech competition** — The industry hasn't been well-served. The bros are building Shopify plugins, not wedding tools.
5. **Client doesn't want to manage it** — Full service, done-for-you. They want it done and want to forget about it.
6. **Specific, enumerable problems** — Not vague. Concrete: "my cards don't match my site," "guests keep texting me," "I don't know my style."
7. **Each problem is AI-solvable** — The sensing identifies the problem. The tech solves it. Minutes, not weeks.
8. **10x improvement is possible** — Not competing on 10% better. Dramatically, obviously better than everything else available.
9. **Built-in community** — Brides talk to brides. Agents talk to agents. One great result becomes referrals.
10. **Clear end point** — You know when it's done. No open-ended scope.

---

**The cross that makes it special:** The sensing layer identifies WHICH problems this specific client has and what "right" looks like for them. The tech layer SOLVES it in minutes. Neither alone works.

*Sensing without tech is just consulting. Tech without sensing is just Squarespace.*

> **Strategy:** Focus on Sphere x Niche that have the highest concentration of these 10 properties. That's where monopoly profits live — not competing on price, but being dramatically, structurally better.
