### The Gap No One Fills

Totally custom to them on PRESENTATION & PROCESS.

Every other service is either: (a) a tool you use yourself (Squarespace, Lovable), or (b) an agency that takes weeks and charges $10K+.

No one occupies the space of: *I sit with you for one hour, pull out what you actually need, and you walk away with a live, coherent, beautiful digital presence that is unmistakably YOU.*

> That's the gap. The eagerly helpful friend problem (Olivia's framing): current AI solves before it listens. "I have this feeling—" "YEAH YEAH I ALREADY DID IT FOR YOU." Every tool on the market does this. You do the opposite.
